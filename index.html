DELUGE

Deluge: Honoring those who honor the process. Deluge delineates those who truly deserve success verse those who stop by to only demean. Deluge is a 1.3% fee from every transaction that is added to liquidity. These fees continue to accumulate until Rectitude is called. Upon calling Rectitude, all liquidity providers are rewarded for their loyalty with these rewards.

RECTITUDE

Rectitude: A unique experience that is available every two hours. Blessed community members whom hold more than 64 ARCH have the ability to call Rectitude wherein 3% of all liquidity is removed. One removed, Ethereum is used to market buy. At this point, ARCH 97% is destroyed forever and 3% is sent to the caller. At call, Deluge is then released into existence.
