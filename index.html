DELUGE

Deluge: Honoring those who honor the process. Deluge delineates those who truly deserve success verse those who stop by to only demean. Deluge is a 1.3% fee from every transaction that is added to liquidity. These fees continue to accumulate until Rectitude is called. Upon calling Rectitude, all liquidity providers are rewarded for their loyalty with these rewards.

RECTITUDE

Rectitude: A unique experience that is available every two hours. Blessed community members whom hold more than 64 ARCH have the ability to call Rectitude wherein 3% of all liquidity is removed. Once removed, Ethereum is used to market buy. At this point, ARCH 97% is destroyed forever and 3% is sent to the caller. At call, Deluge is then released into existence.

REINCARNATION

Reincarnation brings new life to each and every holder of Angel. 5% of each Angel transaction is reborn into different forms, with 2% being born again to each Angel holders wallet where 3% starts anew as liquidity, constantly adding fruitfulness to the pool. We should add, the initial reward wallet (100,000 AGL) also receives the 2% of all transactions, the same as any other AGL holder. This interest is distributed to all of the LP holders of the ANGEL/ETH pair.

REBIRTH

Rebirth operates much like the four yearly cycles, the seasons of the year. Throughout the day, holders will be given to opportunity to call Rebirth to the token where 1% of liquidity is pulled from the liquidity pool. The ETH from this pair is used to market buy AGL. The AGL is rewarded 50% to the caller of this function and the other 50% is burned. This function can only be called once every 8 hours and requires 10,000 AGL to perform.. With rebirth comes death, and both are necessary for financial sustainability. Rebirth keeps supply low and thriving.
